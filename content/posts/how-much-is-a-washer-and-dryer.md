If you are in the market for a new washer and dryer, you may be wondering how much they cost. The answer depends on a few factors, such as the size, type, and features of the washer and dryer.

The average cost of a washer and dryer set ranges from $800 to $2,000. Front-loading washers typically cost more than top-loading models. High-efficiency models also tend to cost more than standard models. Washers with extra features such as steam cycles or larger capacity drums can also add to the cost.

Dryers range in price from $400 to $1,500. Electric dryers tend to cost more than gas models. High-end models with extra features such as steam cycles or larger capacity drums will add to the overall price tag.
