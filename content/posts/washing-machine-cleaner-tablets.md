Washing machine cleaner tablets are specially formulated to clean the inside of your washer without damaging the internal parts or leaving behind any residue. They dissolve easily in water and penetrate deep into the drum, removing dirt, grease, and other build-up that can cause odors. The tablets also help remove residues from detergent and fabric softener that may have built up over time. 

To use the tablets, simply follow the instructions on the package or bottle carefully. Most products require you to place one tablet in an empty washer drum before running a hot cycle (without any clothes). If you have a front-loading washer, you may need to place two tablets instead. After completing the cycle, thoroughly wipe down all surfaces with a damp cloth or paper towel for best results. 

By using washing machine cleaner tablets regularly (once every month or two), you can help keep your washer running smoothly while eliminating bad odors from occurring in the future!
