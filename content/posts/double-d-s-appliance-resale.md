Double D’s Appliance Resale is a family owned business that has been providing quality used appliances to customers for over 25 years. Located in Long Island, New York, Double D’s provides an easy and affordable way for customers to purchase quality used appliances. With a wide selection of all the top brands like Whirlpool, Maytag, GE, and more, Double D’s makes it easy for customers to find the perfect appliance for their home.

At Double D’s Appliance Resale, customers can expect to find gently used appliances that are inspected and tested by experienced professionals before being put on the showroom floor. Everything from refrigerators and dishwashers to washers and dryers can be found at Double D’s. And if you don't see what you're looking for in store, they will try their best to order it in or provide an alternative solution. 

The team at Double D's is dedicated to customer service and satisfaction. They strive to make sure each customer finds the right appliance that meets their needs while staying within their budget. Their knowledgeable staff will help guide you through your options so you can make an informed decision when purchasing your next appliance. 

In addition to sales of quality used appliances, Double D's also offers repair services for all makes and models of major household appliances as well as extended warranties on select products purchased from them (ask about availability). So whether you are in need of a new refrigerator or are looking for repair services on your existing one - Double D's Appliance Resale has got you covered!
