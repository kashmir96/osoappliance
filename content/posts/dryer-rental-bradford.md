Dryers are essential pieces of equipment for any household that regularly washes clothes. They help reduce the time spent on drying clothes by removing excess moisture from fabrics more quickly than air drying alone. Dryers also protect garments against potential shrinkage or damage from over-drying, resulting in longer lasting clothing items. 

When renting a dryer from Bradford you can choose from a variety of models, including both vented and condenser options. Vented machines draw air out of the drum through an external vent hose and are typically used for large loads or larger pieces of clothing such as bedding or towels. Condenser models recirculate air back into the drum using a fan and water reservoir – this type is often used for smaller loads and lighter fabrics such as underwear or t-shirts due to its gentler drying cycle.
