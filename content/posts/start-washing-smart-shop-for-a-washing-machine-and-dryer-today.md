## Shop Smart: Find the Right Washer and Dryer

Washing clothes is an unavoidable part of life, but with the right machine, it doesn't have to be a chore. Shopping smart for a washing machine and dryer will save you time and money in the long run, while still getting clothes clean and free of wrinkles. 

Whichever type of household appliance you choose, you have to consider the level of cleaning you need, the size of the machine, type of features, and the amount of money you’re willing to spend. Washing machines come with two main energy ratings: A++ for energy efficiency and A for water efficiency. Consider how much water and electricity you want to use each month, and select your washing machine accordingly. 

When selecting a dryer, you’ll want to look at the size of the drum and the energy rating. Larger drums are able to handle bigger loads, meaning you can cut down on the labor. Make sure you select the right one for your family’s size and needs to avoid wasting energy and time.

When it comes to making a decision, you'll want to read reviews and even take a look at our [Overview of Top Appliances](./pages/appliance-overview) to get a clearer picture of what is out there. After selecting the right washer and dryer, you’ll experience the convenience of washing clothes efficiently and quickly. 

Getting the right washing machine and dryer will be worth the investment. Not only can you save time and money, but you can also guarantee your clothes come out looking great each time.
