A clothes dryer is an appliance used to dry wet laundry. The cost of a clothes dryer can vary greatly depending on the type, size, and features you are looking for. 

The most basic types of dryers are vented models that use a heating element and a fan to blow hot air through the drum. These models typically cost between $200 and $600. 

If you want a more advanced model, you can opt for a condenser dryer, which uses hot air to evaporate the moisture from your laundry. These models are generally more expensive than vented models, with prices ranging from $500 to over $1,000. 

You can also get heat pump dryers, which use heat pumps to generate hot air and recycle it back into the drum. These models are usually the most expensive option, with prices ranging from $1,000 to over $2,000. 

For those who live in apartments or condos without access to outdoor vents for regular vented models, there are ventless dryers available as well. These models use condensation or heat pump technology to evaporate moisture from your laundry without needing an external venting system. Ventless dryers are usually more expensive than other types of dryers, with prices ranging from $800 to over $1,500.
