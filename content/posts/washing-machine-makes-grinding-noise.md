First, check the drum of your washing machine for any foreign objects like coins or buttons. If you find anything, remove it carefully. You should also check the filter for any blockages and clear them out if necessary.

Next, examine the hoses of your washing machine for any kinks or bulges which may indicate a blockage in the line. If so, replace these hoses with new ones and see if this resolves the issue.

Finally, make sure that all of the connections between the appliance and its water source are secure and that no water is leaking out anywhere.
