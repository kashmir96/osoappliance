## Can You Take Dogs to Laundromats? A Look at Pet-Friendly Laundry Services 

Do you ever wish that your pup could be by your side when doing the laundry? The good news is, there are pet-friendly laundromats available in some places. It's important to first check with the local laundromat's rules and regulations before bringing your furry friend in. 

Most establishments will allow small pets such as cats and dogs, but this may vary based on state laws or other regulations. If a pet is allowed inside, they should remain leashed or crated while you wait for the laundry cycle to finish. Some places even go as far as providing toys, treats, and water dishes for four-legged customers! 

In addition to traditional laundromats being pet-friendly, there are many mobile laundry services that offer pick up and drop off services right at your doorstep! These businesses often have their own rules regarding pets - typically allowing only service animals in the vehicle - but it’s worth looking into if you don’t have time to make it to a brick-and mortar location.
