Steam on a washing machine is a feature that has become increasingly popular in recent years. It is a feature that can be found on many modern washing machines and is used to help reduce wrinkles and odors in clothes.

When steam is used in a washing machine, it helps to loosen dirt and grime from fabrics before the washing cycle begins. This helps to make sure that the clothes are thoroughly cleaned. Steam also helps to reduce wrinkles in clothes, making them look fresher and more presentable.

Steam can also be used to reduce odors in clothes. The steam helps to break down odors and bacteria, leaving clothes smelling fresher and cleaner. This is especially useful for items such as gym clothes or work uniforms that tend to accumulate odors over time.

Overall, steam on a washing machine is a great feature that can help to make laundry day a little easier. It helps to reduce wrinkles and odors in clothes, making them look and smell better. It also helps to make sure that clothes are thoroughly cleaned, so you can be sure that your clothes are always looking their best.
