Another common issue with fridges is a lack of cooling or no cold air coming out at all. This could be caused by a broken thermostat or compressor failure due to overuse or age-related wear and tear. If your fridge isn’t cooling properly, it’s important to call a professional technician right away so they can diagnose and repair any underlying problems before further damage occurs.
