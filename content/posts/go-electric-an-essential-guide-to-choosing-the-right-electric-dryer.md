## Finding the Perfect Electric Dryer: The Ultimate Guide

Making the switch from a gas-powered to an electric-powered dryer can seem overwhelming. What's the best model for your family? What about size and capacity? Are there important features to consider? Don't worry, below, we will review all you need to know before purchasing an electric dryer. 

One of the most important considerations when buying a dryer is size. You want to make sure the size of the dryer fits into your space. Furthermore, the bigger the capacity, the better the dryer is able to tackle larger loads. Those with a larger family or who usually do bulk laundry will want to consider a larger capacity model. 

In addition to size and capacity, features are also an important consideration. High-end dryers, often with steam and wrinkle-protection functions, are worth considering to make laundry day much easier. Advanced features vary widely by model and manufacturer, so shop around to find the exact features you want.
