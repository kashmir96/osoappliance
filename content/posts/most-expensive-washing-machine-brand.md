# Most expensive washing machine brand

When it comes to finding a reliable and efficient washing machine, there are many brands to choose from. But if you’re looking for the most expensive washing machine brand out there, then you’ll need to be prepared to spend a bit more money. The most expensive washing machine brand on the market is Miele.

Miele has been around since 1899 and is a German-based manufacturer of high-end appliances, including washers and dryers. Miele's top-of-the-line washers feature superior technology that will make sure your clothes get clean on the first try. These washers also boast an A+++ energy rating and are designed with low water consumption in mind, making them both environmentally friendly and cost-efficient in the long run. Many of Miele's designs come with added features such as prewash settings, automatic heat control, steam options, wool care wash cycles and more. Plus they come with either a five or 10 year warranty so you can rest easy knowing your investment is protected for years to come! 

Of course all this premium technology comes at a price - Miele's washer models range from around $1,000 - $3,500 USD depending on size and features included. So if you’re looking for a durable washing machine that won't break down after just one use, then investing in Miele may be worth it for the long haul!
