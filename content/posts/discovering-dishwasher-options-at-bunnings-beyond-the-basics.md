## Evaluating Dishwasher Options at Bunnings to Select the Best
If you're on the lookout for a new dishwasher at Bunnings, you've come to the right place. With all of the options available today, it can be challenging to determine which dishwasher is right for you. That's why we've put together this guide to help you understand what features and aspects to consider when looking for a new dishwasher and how to identify the best dishwasher for you.

The best dishwasher option for you will depend on your lifestyle and needs. If you have a large family, you'll want something with a bigger capacity, while those with smaller families or couples could go for a more compact model. Additionally, the types of cycles it has, the number of racks and baskets, the height of the unit, the water consumption, and the noise it produces are all factors to consider.

When choosing a dishwasher, look for one with special options like a 60-minute express cycle for when you need a quick rinse, an eco-friendly wash, and adjustable racks for loading a variety of dishes and utensils. Additionally, you'll want to find one with a range of temperature settings, as well as a delay-start option that allows you to conveniently run your dishwasher at a time that suits you.

Finally, make sure you take into consideration the cost of the dishwasher as well as any services or warranties that come with it. Many manufacturers offer a warranty on parts and labour, while some dishwasher brands offer their own extended warranties.
