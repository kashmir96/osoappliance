The first thing to consider when looking at commercial washing machine prices is the size of the machine. Generally speaking, larger machines are more expensive than smaller ones. You should also consider how many loads the machine can handle simultaneously; this will determine how quickly you can do your laundry and can help drive up or down the cost. Another important factor is whether or not the machine has extra features such as an automatic shutoff timer or temperature control settings.
