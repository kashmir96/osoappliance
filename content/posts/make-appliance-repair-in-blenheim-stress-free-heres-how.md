Once you determine which appliance needs repair, take a few minutes to create a plan. Use the Internet to research the appliance and its expected lifespan. Then make a plan that clearly outlines when and how you will handle repairs and the potential costs of repair.

Appliances are made up of multiple parts, and finding the right ones is key. Make sure you have a good understanding of the specific make and model before you purchase any parts, as this will save you time and money in the long run.
