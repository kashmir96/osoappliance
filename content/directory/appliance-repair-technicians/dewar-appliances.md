Dewar Appliances is an Appliance Repair Technician Company operating in Auckland, New Zealand.

This business offers a range of appliance repair services to both households and businesses located in and around Auckland.

This company services all of the following brands: Fisher and Paykel, Simpson, Westinghouse, Samsung, Smeg, Classique, Baumatic, Ariston, Indesit, LG, Haier, Parmco, Delonghi, Mitsubishi, Bosch, Electrolux.

To check out this company you can view their website here: dewarappliances.co.nz/book-a-job/.
