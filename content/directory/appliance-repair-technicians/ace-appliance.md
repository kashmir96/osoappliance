# Ace Appliance is an Appliance Repair Technician Company operating in Wellington, New Zealand.

This business offers a range of appliance repair services to both households and businesses located in and around Wellington.

This company services all of the following brands: Samsung, Mitsubishi, Simpson, Smeg, Bellini, LG, Ariston, Haier, Westinghouse, Classique, Asko, Fisher & Paykel, Daewoo, Beko, Baumatic, Delonghi, Electrolux, Miele, Bosch

To check out this company you can view their website here: www.aceappliance.co.nz/.
