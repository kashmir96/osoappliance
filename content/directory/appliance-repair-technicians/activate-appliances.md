Activate Appliances is an Appliance Repair Technician Company operating in Auckland, New Zealand.

This business offers a range of appliance repair services to both households and businesses located in and around Auckland.

This company services all of the following brands: Asko, Award, Bosch, Fisher & Paykel, Haier, Elba, Simpson, Westinghouse, Electrolux, Smeg, Baumatic, Classique, Falcon, Rangemaster, Mitsubishi, Trieste, Indesit

To check out this company you can view their website here: www.activateappliances.co.nz/.
