# S and J Appliance is an Appliance Repair Technician Company operating in Auckland, New Zealand.

This business offers a range of appliance repair services to both households and businesses located in and around Auckland.

This company services all of the following brands: AEG, Asko, Maytag, Bosch, Electrolux, Fisher & Paykel, Panasonic, Simpson, Smeg, Westinghouse
