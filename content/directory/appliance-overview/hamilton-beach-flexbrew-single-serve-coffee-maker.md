## An overview of the Hamilton Beach FlexBrew Single Serve Coffee Maker Coffee Machine:

If you’re in the market for a single serve coffee maker, then you should definitely consider the Hamilton Beach FlexBrew Single Serve Coffee Maker. This machine is perfect for those who are looking for a simple and convenient way to make one cup of coffee at a time.

The Hamilton Beach FlexBrew Single Serve Coffee Maker has two brewing options: regular ground coffee or K-Cup packs. With its adjustable cup rest, you can use either a standard size mug or your favorite travel mug that fits up to a 14-ounce capacity. The machine also features an auto-shutoff feature so you don’t have to worry about leaving it on all day long. Additionally, the water reservoir can hold up to 10 ounces of water so you don’t have to fill it up constantly. 

In terms of convenience, this machine is top notch! It comes with an auto-pause and serve feature so that you can remove your cup before the entire brew cycle is complete without any dripping or messes being made. Plus, the removable drip tray makes cleaning easy and hassle free - simply empty out the tray after each use and wipe it down with a damp cloth if necessary. Lastly, this machine has an illuminated power button that glows blue when powered on - making it easy to spot in your kitchen! 

Overall, if you’re looking for an affordable single serve coffee maker with plenty of convenience features then look no further than the Hamilton Beach FlexBrew Single Serve Coffee Maker!
