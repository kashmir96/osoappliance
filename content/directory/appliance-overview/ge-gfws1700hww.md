## An overview of the GE GFWS1700HWW Washing machine:

The GE GFWS1700HWW washing machine is a top-of-the-line, front loading washer that offers powerful cleaning and convenience features. It features an Energy Star rating, meaning it meets the energy efficiency standards set by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). This model is designed to be both efficient and reliable while providing excellent cleaning results. 

One of the most notable features of this model is its 5.8 cu ft capacity drum, which can hold up to 17 full size towels or other large items at once for maximum efficiency during laundry day tasks. Additionally, the unit includes 12 wash cycles that are preprogrammed for specific fabric types such as delicates and heavy duty fabrics as well as activewear. Further customization options are available via the included SmartDispense Plus technology which allows you to add detergent directly into your washer before it begins its cycle in order to get optimal cleaning results based on different fabrics and soil levels. 

The GE GFWS1700HWW also comes with a number of additional convenience features such as steam clean technology which helps reduce wrinkles while preventing shrinkage of clothing items during wash cycles; deep fill capability allowing you to add extra water if needed; delay start option so you can schedule when your laundry will start; and advanced vibration control which helps reduce noise levels when in use for a more peaceful environment in your home or apartment complex. 

Overall, the GE GFWS1700HWW washing machine offers superior performance combined with energy efficiency and modern conveniences that make laundry day tasks easier than ever before!
